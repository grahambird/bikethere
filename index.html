BikeThere! Cardiff

Route 1 Llanishen shops to City Hall

A meandering ride from North Cardiff to the city centre. Mostly flat.

  • Meet at

    The Hub, Llanishen

  • Departing

    8am, Mondays

  • Distance

    4.75 miles

  • Time

    30 mins

Starting on Llanishen high street, this route goes past the Heath train stations and along the edge of Heath Park. Then it goes along the path by the hospital to Gabalfa interchange before joining North Road. Finally onto the Taff Trail for a short stretch into Bute Park before emerging at the Royal Welsh College of Music and Drama and little way further to City Hall.
